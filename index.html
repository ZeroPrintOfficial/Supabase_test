Connect Your Google Account

ZeroPrint uses Google OAuth 2.0 to securely analyze your account security settings. You control exactly what we can see.

What we'll analyze (with your permission):

✓
Account Security Settings
2-step verification status, recovery options
✓
Connected Applications
Third-party apps with access to your Google account
✓
Privacy Settings
Ad personalization, location history status
✗
What we will NEVER access
Your emails, contacts, documents, photos, or any private content — ever.
⚙️
Developer Configuration Required

This integration requires Google OAuth credentials. Add to your .env file:

GOOGLE_CLIENT_ID=your_client_id_here
GOOGLE_CLIENT_SECRET=your_client_secret_here
GOOGLE_API_KEY=your_api_key_here
